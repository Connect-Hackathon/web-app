Before you choose your skill level

Make sure you choose the correct level, and be sure that you match the following criteria before submitting. Otherwise you may not be able to play on the day

  • Level A: High Performance : Category only for University and Club 1st and 2nd team, Members with Badminton Wales/England ranking and grading above G
  • Level B: Competitive : Excluding players above grade H
  • Level C: Beginners and Intramural Players
